    HMRC info

    National Insurance

    You pay mandatory National Insurance if you’re 16 or over and are either:

    • an employee earning above £184 a week or
    • self-employed and making a profit of £6,515 or more a year

    You stop paying when:

    • you reach the state pension age (employed or self employed or unemployed) or
    • you aren't earning enough or
    • you die!

    Student Loans

  • Plan 1 = Pay when income is over £382 a week, £1,657 a month or £19,895 a year (before tax and other deductions).
  • Plan 2 = Pay when income is over £524 a week, £2,274 a month or £27,295 a year (before tax and other deductions).
  • Plan 4 = Pay when income is over £480 a week, £2,083 a month or £25,000 a year (before tax and other deductions).
  • Post graduate loan = Pay when income is over £403 a week, £1,750 a month or £21,000 a year (before tax and other deductions).
